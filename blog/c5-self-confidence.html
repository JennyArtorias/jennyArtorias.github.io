What is the relationship between mindfulness and self awareness?

Mindfulness training and techniques develop our analytical awareness to complement our subjective self awareness to compelete this wisdom.

Self awareness gives us an understanding of what we are doing, our strengths and weaknesses, of our likes and dislikes, of our own personality, of our emotions it is subjective.

Mindfulness gives us realisation and recall. it is a form of awareness, but is analytical giving us observations without judgement. It is objective.
